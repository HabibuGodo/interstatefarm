Interstate Farm ltd is based and registered in Tanzania since November 2016 as an agro-business company. Inter State Farm provides comprehensive business solutions to smallholder coffee-farmers in Tanzania by ensuring that they use modern production methods and support them with farming inputs so that they increase coffee returns and ensure the best quality of coffee. The company specifically aim at ensuring there is fair value along the coffee supply chain from the farm to the final coffee consumer. Transparent is the key at all level of the supply chain which enable farmers to get the fair value of their produces which the company buys in an open and fair agreement with the farmers then coffee beans are processed, packed for distribution/sales within the country and export.
